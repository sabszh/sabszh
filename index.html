About Me

I’m currently pursuing a Cand.it in Cognitive Science at Aarhus University, with a deep fascination for how humans and technology interact—particularly when it comes to how our brains behave when put in front of these cutting-edge tech. What truly excites me is witnessing how my work can evoke new experiences in others, especially when technology serves as the mediator.

In various student work roles, I’ve explored AI safety, robotics, and the intersection of research and art, helping a research group integrate AI to expand the possibilties of their projects. I also co-founded a startup with two friends, where we experiment with using LLMs to enhance learning and study experiences. Time and again, I’m reminded that the intersection of humans and technology is where I find my creative spark. Thanks for taking the time to read!
